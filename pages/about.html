About BenchPressPRO

Our Mission

At BenchPressPRO, our mission is to provide fitness enthusiasts and strength trainers with the most accurate and user-friendly bench press calculator available. We believe in empowering individuals to track their progress and set realistic goals in their strength training journey.

Why Choose BenchPressPRO?

  • Accuracy: We use multiple established formulas to calculate your 1RM, ensuring the most precise estimate possible.
  • User-Friendly: Our intuitive interface makes it easy for anyone to use, from beginners to seasoned lifters.
  • Comprehensive Information: Beyond just calculations, we provide valuable information about bench press techniques, standards, and muscle engagement.
  • Accessibility: With our responsive design and dark mode feature, you can use BenchPressPRO comfortably on any device, day or night.
